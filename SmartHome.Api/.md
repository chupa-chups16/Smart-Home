## 10) Update 2026-02-28 - Loi may khac khong goi duoc API
- Trieu chung:
  - Swagger tren may khac bao `Failed to fetch`.
  - Ly do thuong gap: CORS, network, hoac redirect HTTP -> HTTPS.
- Nguyen nhan trong project:
  - `Program.cs` chua cau hinh CORS cho frontend/may khac.
  - Co `UseHttpsRedirection()` gay redirect sang HTTPS, may khac de fail do cert dev.
  - Port co the bi chiem (`address already in use`).
- Da fix:
  - Them CORS policy `LanPolicy` trong `Program.cs`.
  - Them `app.UseCors("LanPolicy")`.
  - Chi dung `UseHttpsRedirection()` khi khong phai `Development`.
  - Doi `Properties/launchSettings.json`:
    - Tu: `https://0.0.0.0:7157;http://0.0.0.0:7156`
    - Thanh: `http://0.0.0.0:7156`
- Cach test tu may khac:
  - Mo `http://<LAN_IP>:7156/swagger/index.html`
  - Vi du: `http://192.168.1.53:7156/swagger/index.html`
- Luu y van hanh:
  - Neu bao `address already in use`, kiem tra process dang chiem cong 7156/7157.
  - Mo firewall inbound cho port 7156 tren may chay API.
  - Dam bao API bind `0.0.0.0` de may khac trong LAN truy cap duoc.
